About Us

We are Re-Cycle. We are a small company located in Oslo specializing in guided bike tours caring about saving the environment one trip at a time. Either you are new in town, just want some exercise or are interested in dicovering new sides of your city, we are here for you. We offer six different concepts and themes for the rides and all food served will be ecological and organic.
